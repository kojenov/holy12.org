### Ingredients

- 1 1/2 c whole wheat flour
- 1/4 c sugar
- 1/4 c brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 ripe bananas
- 2 strawberries chopped (optional)
- 1/3 c apple sauce/ prune paste
- 1/2 c chocolate chips
- 1/4 c cocoa (optional)
- 1/4 c oil
- 1/4 c soy milk
- 1/4 c nuts/ raisins/ oats

### Directions

350F, 1 hour
