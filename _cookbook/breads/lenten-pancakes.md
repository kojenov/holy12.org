### Ingredients

- 2c flour
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2c soy milk
- 3 tbsp oil
- 1/2 tsp vanilla

### or

- 2c flour
- 1c whole wheat flour
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 2 tbsp baking powder
- 3c soy milk
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 4 1/2 tbsp oil
